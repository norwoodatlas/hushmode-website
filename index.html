Frequently Asked Questions

What is Hush Mode?

Hush Mode is an iOS focus app that helps reduce screen time by adding a short pause before selected distracting apps.

Is Hush Mode a screen time control app?

Yes. It uses iOS Screen Time capabilities to support mindful usage patterns and fewer impulsive app opens.

Who should use Hush Mode?

People who want better focus, less doomscrolling, and a practical digital wellbeing system that can actually stick.
