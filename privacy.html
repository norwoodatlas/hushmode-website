Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 25, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Hush Mode ("we", "our", or "us") handles information when you use the Hush Mode iOS app.

Information We Collect

  • App preferences and session data needed to provide core Hush Mode features.
  • Subscription status to unlock paid features.
  • No direct sale of personal information.

How Information Is Used

  • To provide and improve focus-session, shielding, and recap functionality.
  • To determine feature access for free and paid tiers.
  • To maintain app reliability and troubleshoot issues.

On-Device Data

Hush Mode is designed to keep core usage data on-device where possible, including settings and recap history.

Third-Party Services

Hush Mode may rely on third-party services to operate parts of the app:

  • Apple (App Store purchases and platform services)
  • RevenueCat (subscription management)

Data Retention

Data stored in the app remains available until you delete it in-app or remove the app from your device.

Children's Privacy

Hush Mode is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material updates will be reflected with a revised effective date.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact us through the support channel listed on the Hush Mode App Store page.
